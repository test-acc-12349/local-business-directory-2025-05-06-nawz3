Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Local Business Directory. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our directory website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Directory Listings

  • Business name and contact information
  • Business owner details
  • Business description and categories
  • Photos and media content
  • Operating hours and location data

2.2 User Data

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials
  • Usage data and preferences
  • IP address and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain and improve our directory services
  • To communicate with you about your listings or account
  • To process your transactions and requests
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These may include:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand usage patterns
  • Advertising cookies for relevant content

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of incorrect data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@directory.com
